# Song Mute

Mute specific Old School RuneScape music tracks without turning music off entirely.

Song Mute lets you build a personal mute list for songs you do not want to hear. When one of those tracks starts, the plugin temporarily mutes music. When a normal track starts again, your previous music volume is restored automatically.

## Features

- Searchable sidebar list that matches the in-game Music Player track list
- Mute or unmute songs directly from the Song Mute sidebar
- Right-click any song in the in-game Music tab to toggle `Mute` or `Unmute`
- Muted songs are grayed out in the in-game Music list
- Currently playing song is highlighted in the sidebar
- `All tracks` and `Muted tracks` filters for easier list management
- `Clear muted` action to remove the entire mute list quickly
- Saved mute list that persists through normal RuneLite use

## How To Use

1. Install `Song Mute` from the RuneLite Plugin Hub.
2. Open the Song Mute sidebar from the client toolbar.
3. Search for a song and tick its checkbox to mute it.
4. Open the in-game Music tab to see muted songs grayed out.
5. Right-click songs in the Music tab if you want to mute or unmute them there instead.

## Notes

- The plugin only includes songs that appear in the normal in-game Music Player.
- Muting a song takes effect immediately, even if that song is already playing.
- The plugin restores your saved music volume when a muted song ends, you unmute it, or you leave the logged-in game state.
- If you edit muted track IDs manually in config, invalid or unavailable IDs may be removed automatically.
